COMPANY HISTORY

PT. Yogma Wijaya Kusuma has been established since 1999 and has developed into a PT. Yogma Wijaya Kusuma on December 1, 2014. This company is engaged in trading and services based on the fulfillment of the needs for the procurement of construction service equipment. Our business is engaged in the repair of consumables and office equipment suppliers, building construction, renovation, maintenance, blacksmithing, etc. To provide the best service to customers, we have a professional and qualified work team in the field of welding and painting that produces products according to orders, competitive prices and timely completion of work and delivery of goods. For that we hope that if your company needs our company to handle the work in accordance with our field, we will try to solve it as best as possible with maximum results.
